# Discussion on Requirements and Plans: Rethinking the Entire Project

First, we reviewed the entire project and its requirements to clarify our objectives. We confirmed that our primary task is to develop a HarmonyOS-based program that retrieves raw data from a Bluetooth device and transmits it to the S2 and M1 teams.

Second, we discussed the system design to determine the necessary technology stack and development approach.

Third, we agreed on the need to coordinate with the S2 and M1 teams to finalize the data exchange interfaces and required data formats.

Finally, we outlined our action plan for the next phase. Developers will research HarmonyOS Bluetooth development to create a working demo for testers and other groups. Testers will conduct validation using real-world devices to ensure program stability. Meanwhile, Project Managers (PMs) will maintain ongoing communication with other teams to track overall progress.
